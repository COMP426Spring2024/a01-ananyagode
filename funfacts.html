Top 10 Facts About Zeus

  • Zeus is the king of the gods in Greek mythology.
  • He rules the sky and controls weather phenomena.
  • Zeus's symbol is the thunderbolt, which he hurls at those who displease him.
  • He is married to Hera, the queen of the gods, but is famous for his many affairs.
  • Zeus is father to many gods and heroes, including Athena, Apollo, Artemis, and Hercules.
  • His parents are the Titans Cronus and Rhea.
  • Zeus led the Olympian gods in a ten-year war against the Titans, known as the Titanomachy.
  • He was raised in secrecy to prevent his father Cronus from swallowing him as he had done with Zeus's siblings.
  • The eagle is sacred to Zeus, and it often serves as his emblem.
  • Zeus's Roman equivalent is Jupiter, who shares many of the same attributes.
